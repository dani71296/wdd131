Types of Motorcycles

There are several categories based on use and design. Here are the main ones:

Sport Motorcycles

Designed for speed and maneuverability. Ideal for track and agile driving.

Cruiser Motocycles

Comfortable and classic in style, perfect for long trips and relaxing walks.

Touring Motorcycles

Equipped for long journeys with luggage space and great comfort.

Scooters

Compact and easy to handle, ideal for the city and short trips.
